Battlebots

Building a battlebot was by far my hardest engineering project. This involved weeks of research and design before we could even attempt to put together the bot. Once we actually got the parts, we started building the frame, and although framing the electronics was the easy part, it took valuable time. Once we got the frame together, I spent about a week soldering together a power distribution board to a wire converter, allowing us to use one lithium ion battery for the entire bot. However, during our last three weeks of electronics work, we realised the motors we picked out didn’t have enough torque to actually move our bot, all of our hard work put into an immobile hunk of metal as sad as a moldy pickle. This was, by far, my greatest failure.
