Towers

For the first engineering project I did in the year of 2017, I built a balsa wood tower to compete in an engineering class competition. My team consisted of Sameer, Sai, and Matthew, each supplying his own skills to the team. Before this project, we researched the type of joints and braces we should use, comparing the ultimate strength and rotational strength of each, as well as the shear modulus of the material we were using. We decided on using X-Bracing, with butt joints, deciding it was the strongest method. This project taught me the importance of research and teamwork, also giving me experience to better build my engineering projects.
