Introduction

What is engineering? Engineering is a branch of science and technology concerned with design and building. What interests me is the hands-on application of math and physics, which, by themselves, seem fairly plain. I enjoy building and designing technology with practical applications, and I plan to do that as a career. When I create a project I, feel a rush of serotonin. I begin to smile as I marvel at my work, especially so if I spent a lot of time on it. I would like to build something that could impact the world, but I have my whole life ahead of me. Even though I'm still young, I'd like to share some of my experiences. They include the building of a balsa wood tower, building of my computer, design and creation of a “battlebot”, and my experiences in programming.
