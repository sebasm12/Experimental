Conclusion

In conclusion, engineering is my passion. However, there are many branches of engineering, so making my career choice is not over just yet. I’m thinking of going into mechanical engineering, due to its wide range of projects and visible result. And, although I appreciate the hands-on work mechanical engineering will give me, computer engineering (software engineering) has the potential to be one of the better-paying career paths (think Bill Gates, who made Windows). I guess I still have a long way until college, and a way longer life ahead of me. Who knows what it will hold in store for me. I guess I won’t know until it happens. I can’t control a variety of things that could happen to me, so I will try to make the best of it.
